Single Point Crankless Press (NCL)

Application
Applicable to large type of heavy tonnage die tooling with multiple precision stages in blanking, piercing, bending, forming for single or progressive application.

Features

  • Hydraulic overload protection system
  • Extra long 8-points guide plate
  • Air counter balancer device
  • Automatic recirculating lubrication with distributing monitor device
